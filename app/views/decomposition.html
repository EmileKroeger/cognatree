The Data

The etymologies are from Etymological Wordnet, which itself was extracted from Wiktionary data.

Some caveats:

  • Only words in the top 20 000 English words are used.
  • Only simple etymologies where a word has a single ancestor are taken into account; words derived from several words (telescope) or from a word in the same language are not taken into account.
  • There are a few mistakes in the data - either from Wikipedia or the algorithms extracting the data from it; for example Pluto and Uranus are marked as being Malay words.

The data was processed in Python and displayed with d3, using a modified version of the sankey plugin.
